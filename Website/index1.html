What We Do

Are you tired of friends backing out of bets after finding out they lost? We aim to facilitate betting and create a more concrete version of it, such that all participants adhere to the requirements of the bet.

How it Works

Once you login using your Facebook account, your interactions with the chatbot will be stored in a history, which you can access through the ""History"" tab. This tab will contain information regarding the date of the bet, the nature of the bet, the amount bet, and the winner. Money is either transferred into your account or taken out of your account depending on if you win or lose.
